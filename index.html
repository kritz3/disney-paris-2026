Philosophy

This is a priority framework, not a checklist.

  1. Maximalist list, not curated. Better to see something and skip it than not know it was an option.
  2. Scores drive in-park decisions. When tired or behind, drop by score — don't deliberate in the moment.
  3. Times are anchors, not deadlines. 20 min behind is data, not failure.
  4. Pre-committed decisions beat in-the-moment ones. Phantom Manor as Monday's slip, ToT opt-in via POV viewing, Crush's PA at 09:25.
  5. Microbreaks and skips aren't failures. Shade + cold drink is a tactic.
  6. Family state > ride throughput. Especially Tue evening (easy magic) and Wed (victory lap).

Rider Switch tactics

PA + Rider Switch stack (biggest win) Parent A buys PA → rides via PA queue → asks Cast Member for Rider Switch pass → Parent B uses RS fast lane. Cuts AFF + ToT from 80–90 min to ~40 min.

Indy (8yo can't ride)

Use Single Rider line, not Rider Switch. Both adults go through SR separately; ~15 min solo-with-3-kids per shift.

Hyperspace / RC Racer / AFF (8yo rides)

Classic Rider Switch — one parent + 8yo ride, swap, other parent + 8yo ride. 8yo gets to go twice if he wants.

Family rides (Crush's, Frozen, Ratatouille)

One parent stays with baby while the other rides with the boys, then swap. More "baby supervision" than classic RS.

Drop order when slipping

  1. Walkthroughs (Robinson, Aladdin, Castle/Dragon)
  2. Rerides of family rides already done
  3. Spinners (other than Dumbo)
  4. Phantom Manor (designated slip — Wed evening fallback)
  5. Indy (bonus, not core)
  6. Adult-only rides: RC Racer → Tower of Terror → Indy → Hyperspace → AFF
  7. Pinocchio / Snow White
  8. Cars Road Trip
  9. Buzz reride / Spider-Man reride

Real-time canaries

  • 30+ min behind anchor → cut next item or PA the following
  • Family snapping → 15 min shade + hydration reset
  • Baby crying inconsolably twice in 30 min → wrap session
  • 6yo "my legs hurt" → you are already 20 min late. Sit NOW.
  • 8yo refusing to walk → end session within 20 min

Logistics

Santa Fe transit

Use the free hotel shuttle, not the walk. Riverside path is unshaded — brutal at 35°C with stroller. Walk only as fallback if shuttle wait >15 min.

Food

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Perishable lunches eaten at hotel during midday break — food safety in heat
  • Shelf-stable snacks in-park: granola bars, fruit, jerky, bottled milk
  • Tue exception: A/C snack in-park ~12:30, full lunch at hotel post-15:00

Baby Care Centers

  • Disneyland Park: next to Plaza Gardens
  • Adventure World: near Studio 1 entrance

Stroller heat

Damp muslin draped over canopy (never sealed). Park in shade.

Daily bag

Portable charger · refillable water bottles · cooling towels (damp) · sunscreen, hats · baby supplies, wipes, spare clothes · snacks, electrolyte sachets · ponchos if forecast.
